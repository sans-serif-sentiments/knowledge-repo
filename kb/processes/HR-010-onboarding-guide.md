## Overview
A guided path for new hires and their managers covering Day 0, Day 1, Week 1, Day 30, and Day 90. Links out to IT setup, benefits, required training, and manager expectations.

## Timeline
- **Day 0 (pre-start):** Complete paperwork, identity verification, direct deposit, and background checks. Confirm start date/time, team, and manager. Receive laptop shipping details and Okta invite.
- **Day 1:** Account activation (Okta, email, chat), MFA setup, laptop encryption check, mandatory security/HR acknowledgments, join orientation call.
- **Week 1 (Day 7):** Join team rituals, read role playbook, schedule 1:1s, review policies (CO-001/002/004/005/006, SEC-003/004), complete security training.
- **Day 30:** Review goals with manager, confirm tool access, complete remaining trainings, gather early feedback.
- **Day 90:** Performance check-in, finalize probation review, ensure all mandatory modules are completed.

## Required actions (new hire)
- Activate Okta and set MFA; enroll laptop in device management.
- Complete required trainings: Code of Conduct, Data Handling, Security Basics, AI Usage.
- Sign acknowledgments for comms/privacy/social policies.
- Confirm benefits and payroll details.

## Required actions (manager)
- Ensure laptop/access requests submitted before Day 0.
- Set clear 30/90-day goals; schedule weekly 1:1s and team intros.
- Assign buddy/mentor and share role-specific documentation.
