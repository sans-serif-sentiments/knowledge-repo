## Before start
- Submit laptop and access requests 5+ business days ahead.
- Confirm role-based access groups; request any exceptions in advance.
- Share team overview, role expectations, and first-week schedule.
- Assign buddy/mentor; schedule welcome and 1:1s.

## Week 1
- Align on goals (30/90 days) and success metrics.
- Walk through AI usage, security, comms, and privacy policies.
- Verify all required trainings are assigned and in progress.

## Day 30
- Feedback check-in; adjust goals; confirm tool access is sufficient.
- Review any overdue trainings or acknowledgments.

## Day 90
- Probation review (if applicable); finalize performance plan.
- Gather feedback on onboarding experience; relay to HR Ops.
