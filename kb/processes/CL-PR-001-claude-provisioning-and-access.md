## Steps
1) Request: submit access ticket with business justification and manager approval.
2) Approval: AI Platform + IT Access verify role and data handling needs.
3) Provision: enable SSO/MFA; assign enterprise tenant role; confirm logging.
4) Removal: auto-remove on role change/offboarding; review quarterly.

## Notes
- No shared accounts; least privilege.
- For regulated data access, require explicit approval and redaction controls.
