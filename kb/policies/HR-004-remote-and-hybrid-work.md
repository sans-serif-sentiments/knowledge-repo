## Scope
Applies to all employees requesting remote or hybrid arrangements.

## Rules
- Remote/hybrid requires manager approval and must align with role eligibility.
- Maintain coverage for core hours; update location in HRIS for tax/benefits.
- Follow IT-002 and SEC-003 for device/access/security; use company VPN/SSO.

## Requests
- Submit remote/hybrid request in HRIS; manager + HR review; confirm equipment/support.
