# Summary
Outlines behavior expectations, escalation paths, and HR points of contact for employees across all offices.

# Details
## General Expectations
- Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect regardless of role or location.
- Follow "Security First" guidelines when discussing company topics outside internal channels.
- Log time-off and travel in Workday within 48 hours.

## Reporting Process
1. Document the concern (time, place, people involved).
2. Notify the appropriate HRBP within 24 hours.
3. HR acknowledges within 1 business day and provides next steps.
