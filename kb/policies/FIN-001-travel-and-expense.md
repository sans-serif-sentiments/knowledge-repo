## Scope
Rules for booking travel, using corporate cards, submitting expenses, and required receipts/approvals.

## Key rules
- **Booking:** Use the approved travel platform; economy by default; exceptions need manager + Finance approval.
- **Corporate card:** Use for business-only purchases; no personal spend. Report lost/stolen immediately.
- **Receipts:** Required for all expenses > $25 (or local threshold). Itemized receipts for lodging/meals.
- **Per diem:** Follow regional limits; tips within policy limits.
- **Deadlines:** Submit expenses within 10 business days of trip completion.

## Approvals
- Manager approval required for all expense reports.
- Pre-approval required for international travel, conferences, or expenses > $2,000.

## Non-reimbursable examples
- Personal entertainment, family travel, alcohol outside business meals, fines/penalties, luxury upgrades.
