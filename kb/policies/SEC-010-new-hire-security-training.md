## Requirement
All new hires must complete security training within 14 days of start and recertify annually.

## What’s included
- Data handling and classification
- Phishing and MFA hygiene
- Device and VPN safety
- AI/LLM usage guardrails (see AI-001/AI-002/AI-FAQ-001)

## How to complete
1) Log into the LMS with SSO.
2) Complete the “New Hire Security Fundamentals” module.
3) Attest to code of conduct and data handling acknowledgments.

## Compliance
- Overdue learners are escalated to managers at Day 15; access may be reduced for high-risk apps.
- Proof of completion lives in the LMS transcript.
