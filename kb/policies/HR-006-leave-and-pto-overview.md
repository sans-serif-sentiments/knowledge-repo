## Summary
- PTO accrual and carryover follow local law and contract terms.
- Submit PTO/leave in HRIS; manager approves; notify team for coverage.
- Long leaves (parental/medical) may require documentation; coordinate with HR and payroll.

## Special Cases
- Travel while remote: ensure IT/geo policies allow access; update HRIS location.
- Extended absences: check benefits/eligibility and handoff plans.
