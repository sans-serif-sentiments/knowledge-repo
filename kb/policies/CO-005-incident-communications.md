## Scope
Customer/internal communications during security or AI incidents (ties to SEC-002, AI-PR-002, PR-003).

## Rules
- Single source of truth; timestamped updates; approvals from Security + Comms + Legal for external notes.
- No speculative statements; share only verified facts; include next update timing.
- Templates must be reviewed before sending; archive comms for audit.
