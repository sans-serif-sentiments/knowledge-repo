# Summary
Guidelines for booking travel, submitting expenses, and reimbursement timelines for all departments.

# Details
## Allowances
- Economy flights for trips < 6hrs, Premium Economy approved with VP approval.
- Hotel cap $220/night in tier-1 cities; $160/night tier-2.
- Meals: $75/day domestic, $95/day international (includes tips).

## Submission Steps
1. Capture receipts via Expensify or the Finance Slack bot.
2. Categorize with cost center + project code.
3. Manager approves within 3 business days; Finance audits weekly.
