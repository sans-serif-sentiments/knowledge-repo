## Scope
Applies to all employees for reviews, feedback cycles, and performance improvement.

## Cadence
- Formal reviews twice a year; mid-cycle check-ins encouraged.
- Continuous feedback via manager 1:1s; document key outcomes in HRIS.

## Improvement Plans
- When needed, a written plan with goals, timeline, and checkpoints; HRBP co-signs.
