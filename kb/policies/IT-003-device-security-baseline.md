## Requirements
- Managed device with MDM, disk encryption, screen lock, and endpoint protection.
- OS updates weekly; critical patches within 48 hours.
- VPN/SSO enforced for corporate resources; block rooted/jailbroken devices.

## Ownership
- User reports loss/theft immediately (IT-PR incident); IT verifies compliance periodically.
