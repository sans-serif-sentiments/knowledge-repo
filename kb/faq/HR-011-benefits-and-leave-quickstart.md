## How to enroll
1) Log into the benefits portal (link in welcome email).
2) Complete plan selections within 30 days of start date.
3) Add beneficiaries/dependents; upload required documents if prompted.

## PTO and leave basics
- PTO accrues monthly (see policy HR-006 for details).
- Submit requests via the HR system; manager approval required.
- For medical/parental leave, email benefits@company.com for documentation steps.
