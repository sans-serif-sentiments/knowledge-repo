## How to apply
1) Go to the HRIS/Time-Off app and create a new request.
2) Select leave type (PTO, sick, parental, bereavement, unpaid, jury duty).
3) Choose start/end dates and add context (location if regional holidays differ).
4) Attach required docs (medical/parental proof if applicable).
5) Submit for **manager approval**; HR Ops is auto-notified for exceptions.

## Approvals and SLAs
- Manager approves/denies within 2 business days.
- HR Ops reviews medical/parental/extended leave.
- For >10 consecutive business days, provide coverage plan and contact details.

## Edits and cancellations
- Cancel or amend directly in the HRIS before the start date; after start, email HR Ops.
- If you worked while on approved PTO, adjust the request and notify your manager.

## Emergencies and sick leave
- Notify your manager ASAP and file the request within 24 hours.
- For 3+ days sick leave, HR Ops may request documentation per policy HR-006.
